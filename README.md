### Building the next generation of intelligent avionics systems

Orch Avionics is developing **AI-native flight systems** for general aviation—combining real-time sensor fusion, predictive intelligence, and fully on-device autonomy.

---

## ✈️ Flagship Product

### Orch Avionic 1 — Predictive EFB

An all-in-one Electronic Flight Bag with:

- ADS-B traffic awareness  
- GPS navigation  
- Handheld radio integration  
- Fuel planning & performance tools  
- Synthetic vision powered by on-device AI  

> Designed to anticipate—not just display.

---

## 🧠 What Makes It Different

- **On-device AI**  
  No dependency on internet. Runs locally in the cockpit.

- **Predictive Assistance**  
  Understands traffic patterns and pilot intent in real time.

- **Integrated Hardware + Software**  
  Purpose-built form factor—not a patched stack of apps.

- **Offline-first Aviation Stack**  
  Built for reliability in real-world flight conditions.
